-- Rules --

Chinchirorin is a game of dice traditionally played with three six sided die in a bowl.

    Rules:

  1. Storm / Triples

    1. Three of a kind of 2 - 6 is an automatically win.
    2. Three 1's is an automatic loss.
    3. When a Storm / Triple is thrown you win or lose triple your bet.

  2. Straights

    1. A straight of 1,2,3 is an automatic loss.
    2. A straight of 4,5,6 is an automatic win.
    3. When a Straight is thrown you win or lose double your bet

  3. Doubles

    1. When doubles are thrown then the number of the third die is your score.
    2. The higher of the two scores is awarded the amount of the bet.

  4. Draw

    1. When neither player throws a winning combination the game is a draw.

  5. Assumptions:

    When a player rolls a straight the other player's throw is ignored. It is always assumed that the Bank throws first, therefore, if both players throw either Triples or Straights the Banks's throw is the accepted combination.
